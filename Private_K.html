She isn't interested in me, or maybe I should say that she never was. Before leaving my hostel room I looked in the mirror and made myself promise to stay the same with her after the trip. I know that if I used all my wishes or every prayer known to mankind I wouldn't be able to be with her. I know she sees my as a friend. But I can't see her in that way still.
